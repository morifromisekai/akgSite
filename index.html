Bridging Craftsmanship, Industry, and Education

I am Amit K Gehlot, a product designer and design educator. For over 25 years, I have worked at the intersection of grassroots craft development, commercial product design, and higher education.

Having served as an Associate Professor and Senior Faculty member at institutions like the National Institute of Design, Madhya Pradesh, I have led industrial design departments and shaped foundation curriculums. My professional practice focuses on bringing academic rigor and deep material knowledge directly to the MSME sector and the next generation of creative founders.
